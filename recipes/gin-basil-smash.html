Gin Basil Smash

Ingredients:

  • 12 Basil Leaves
  • 2oz Gin
  • 3/4 oz Lemon Juice
  • 1/3 oz Simple Syrup

Instructions:

  1. Muddle basil and syrup in cocktail shaker
  2. Shake vigorously with gin and lemon juice
  3. Serve over a large rock in double old fashioned
