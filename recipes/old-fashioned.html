Old Fashioned

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Bourbon
  • 1/4 oz Simple Syrup
  • 3 dashes Angostura Bitters
  • Orange Peel
  • Cherry
  • Large Ice Cube

Instructions:

  1. Stir bourbon, syrup, and bitters with ice
  2. Strain into double old fashioned over large ice cube
  3. Wipe the rim with orange peel and garnish with cherry
